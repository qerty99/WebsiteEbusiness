Custom Build

Suited Switch offers a build service where a customer collaborates with our team to create the mechanical keyboard of their dreams.

The form below asks for your preferences for your keyboard such as the size and colours. When submitted it will be sent to our team and we will get in touch with you via email. There will be a consultation period where we further discuss your preferences to come up with a design to your liking. Once you are satisfied, we will source high quality components and assemble your keyboard with care.
